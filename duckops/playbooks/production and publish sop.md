## Source vs. Distribution Architect

|**Folder**|**Purpose**|**Behavior**|
|---|---|---|
|`/vault/source`|**Your Workspace**|Where you use Obsidian Bases, transclusions, and drafting. **Quartz ignores this.**|
|`/vault/content`|**The Output**|The "Flattened" Markdown generated by your plugin. **Quartz parses this.**|
|`/vault/quartz`|**The Engine**|The Zensical/Quartz configuration files.|
